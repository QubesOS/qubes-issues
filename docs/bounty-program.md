# Qubes OS Bounty Program

The Qubes OS project offers bounties for certain issues to incentivize community contributions and accelerate development of important features and bug fixes.

## How Bounties Work

Bounties are offered for specific issues that have been labeled with the `bounty` label in our [GitHub issues repository](https://github.com/QubesOS/qubes-issues). These represent work that the Qubes team has identified as particularly valuable to the project.

### Finding Bounty Issues

You can find all current bounty issues by:
- Browsing issues with the [`bounty` label](https://github.com/QubesOS/qubes-issues/issues?q=is%3Aopen+is%3Aissue+label%3Abounty)
- Checking our [bounty-specific search](https://github.com/QubesOS/qubes-issues/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3Abounty)

### Bounty Process

1. **Find a bounty issue** that interests you and matches your skills
2. **Comment on the issue** to express your interest and ask any clarifying questions
3. **Wait for confirmation** from a Qubes team member before starting work
4. **Submit your solution** as a pull request to the appropriate repository
5. **Receive review** from the Qubes team
6. **Get paid** once your contribution is accepted and merged

## Bounty Guidelines

### Before You Start

- Read our [contributing guidelines](https://doc.qubes-os.org/en/latest/introduction/contributing.html)
- Review the [issue tracking documentation](https://doc.qubes-os.org/en/latest/introduction/issue-tracking.html)
- Make sure you understand the requirements in the bounty issue
- Ask questions if anything is unclear

### Code Quality Requirements

- Follow existing code style and conventions
- Include appropriate tests where applicable
- Update documentation as needed
- Ensure your code builds successfully
- Address all review feedback promptly

### Communication

- Keep the community updated on your progress
- Respond to questions and feedback in a timely manner
- Be respectful and professional in all interactions
- Follow our [code of conduct](https://doc.qubes-os.org/en/latest/introduction/code-of-conduct.html)

## Payment Information

Bounty amounts and payment details are specified in each individual bounty issue. Payment is typically made after:

1. Your pull request is reviewed and approved
2. Your code is merged into the appropriate branch
3. Any additional verification steps are completed

## Third-Party Platforms

The Qubes OS project may occasionally work with external platforms that provide additional infrastructure for bounty management, such as:

- Automated escrow services
- AI-assisted code review
- Enhanced project management tools

When using such platforms, contributors should:
- Ensure they understand the platform's terms of service
- Verify that payment methods are acceptable to them
- Continue to follow Qubes OS project guidelines and standards

## Questions?

If you have questions about bounties, please:
- Comment on the specific bounty issue
- Ask on our [forum](https://forum.qubes-os.org/)
- Join the discussion on our [mailing lists](https://doc.qubes-os.org/en/latest/introduction/support.html)
